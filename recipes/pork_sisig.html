Pork Sisig Recipe

Description

A sizzling dish made from chopped and seasoned pig's head and liver, usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers. It's often served on a hot plate, and variations may include adding an egg on top. Pork Sisig is known for its bold and zesty flavors.

Ingredients

  • Pig's head and liver
  • Calamansi
  • Onions
  • Chili peppers
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Mayonnaise (optional)
  • Egg (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil pig's head and liver until tender, then chop finely.
  2. Sauté onions, garlic, and chili peppers.
  3. Add the chopped meat and season with soy sauce, calamansi, and mayonnaise.
  4. Cook until slightly crispy, then serve hot with an optional raw egg on top.
