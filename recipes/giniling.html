Giniling Recipe

Description

A dish made with ground meat, typically pork or beef, sautéed with garlic, onions, tomatoes, and sometimes potatoes or peas. It's seasoned with soy sauce and fish sauce, and often served with rice.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat (pork or beef)
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Soy sauce
  • Fish sauce
  • Potatoes or peas (optional)

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic, onions, and tomatoes.
  2. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in soy sauce, fish sauce, and potatoes or peas.
  4. Simmer until potatoes are tender.
